We Buy Music

Are you looking to sell your collection? Forever Young Records is always looking to purchase high-quality music and memorabilia. Our professional buyers are on staff during select days, ready to appraise your collection and offer you cash or store credit.

What We're Looking For

We purchase a massive variety of formats and genres, including:

  • Vinyl Records (LPs & 45s)
  • CDs & Cassettes
  • Reel to Reel Tapes
  • Turntables and Audio Equipment
  • Vintage Music Posters and T-Shirts
  • RIAA Gold/Platinum Records and Autographs

We do not typically buy most foreign language albums, classical music, or karaoke.
